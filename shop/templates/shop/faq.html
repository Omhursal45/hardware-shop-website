Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about wholesale quotes, ordering, and delivery.

How do I request a wholesale quote?

Go to Bulk Quote (from the navbar) or use the Get Quote button on any product. Fill in your requirements and we’ll respond within 24 hours.

What information do you need for faster pricing?

  • Product name / category
  • Approximate quantity
  • Project location / delivery destination
  • Any specifications or brand preference

Do you support bulk orders for contractors and developers?

Yes. We work with contractors, developers, and project teams for wholesale procurement and material supply.

Is delivery available across India?

Our sourcing and logistics support pan-India delivery. We confirm delivery timelines after reviewing your enquiry.

How do you handle product availability?

We confirm availability and delivery schedules based on the latest stock/sourcing. If a preferred variant is not available, we suggest suitable alternatives.

Can you provide GST invoicing?

Yes. We provide GST billing as per the quotation and order details.
