Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we collect and use information when you request a wholesale quote or contact our team.

What information we collect

When you submit an enquiry or bulk quote request, we collect your name, phone number, email (if provided), product/category details, quantity, project information, and related messages.

How we use your information

We use your details to respond to your enquiry, prepare quotations, coordinate delivery/logistics, and communicate about your request.

Contact and follow-ups

We may contact you via phone or email based on the information you provide to complete your quotation and next steps.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share necessary details with suppliers/logistics partners only to fulfill your enquiry.

Data retention

Enquiry details are retained for business operations and follow-ups until they are no longer needed for these purposes.

Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, contact our team via the Contact page.
